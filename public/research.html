Research Areas

My research focuses on advancing the field of AI through innovative approaches to machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. I am particularly interested in developing practical applications that bridge the gap between academic research and industry needs.

Current research interests include:

  • Large language models and their applications in business
  • Efficient machine learning for resource-constrained environments
  • Computer vision for retail applications
  • Responsible AI and ethical considerations in enterprise applications
  • Natural language processing for customer service automation
