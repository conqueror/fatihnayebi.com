About Me

Data & AI Leader, Founder, and Assistant Professor

Background

Dr. Fatih Nayebi is a Data & AI leader and founder with extensive experience bridging academic research and real-world business impact. He is the Founder & CEO of OODARIS AI and VP of Data & AI at ALDO Group, leading teams that build practical AI solutions for measurable business value.

With a Ph.D. in Computer Science specializing in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing, Dr. Nayebi has contributed significantly to the field through his research, publications, and teaching at prestigious institutions.

Professional Experience

VP of Data & AI, ALDO Group

Assistant Professor, McGill University
Teaching graduate-level courses in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science, supervising student research projects, and contributing to curriculum development.

Education

Ph.D. in Computer Science - Specialization in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing
Master's in Computer Science - Focus on Artificial Intelligence

Research Interests

Dr. Nayebi's research interests include machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and their applications in business and industry. His work focuses on developing practical AI solutions that can be implemented at scale to solve complex business problems.
